About Fusion Research Facility

Fusion Research Facility is a sci-fi Roblox experience where players explore a high-tech research complex, manage dangerous experiments, and survive unexpected facility events. Work with others, uncover secrets, and keep the facility stable… or watch it collapse.

Game History

The Fusion Research Facility began as a small experimental Roblox project and quickly evolved into a full sci-fi experience. With continuous updates, new systems, and expanding lore, the facility grows more advanced — and more dangerous — over time.
