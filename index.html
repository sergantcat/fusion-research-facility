About The Game

Fusion Research Facility is a sci-fi Roblox experience where players manage, operate, and survive inside a high-tech experimental fusion reactor complex. Work together, prevent meltdowns, and explore the facility.

Game History

The facility began as a small experimental project and has grown into a full-scale research complex. Through many updates and improvements, the game continues to evolve with new systems, areas, and challenges.
